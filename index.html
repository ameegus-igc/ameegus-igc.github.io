The 15th Annual Meeting of Gulbenkian Students (AMeeGuS) will take place in Évora - Alentejo, Portugal - from 31st January to 3rd February 2022.

The event is expected to receive 50+ PhD students from Instituto Gulbenkian de Ciência (IGC) and ITQB, 2 Keynote Speakers and several Principal Investigators from IGC.

Bus transportation will be provided by IGC to take participants from the institute to the venue. Details about transportation and the programme will be provided to the participants via email, closer to the date of the event. We are going to include all covid safety measures (e.g. quick covid tests will be provided to everyone before entering the bus).

About AMeeGuS

AMeeGuS is the traditionally annual meeting of the PhD students from Instituto Gulbenkian de Ciência (IGC), an international centre for biomedical and biological research and graduate training, that has been ranked among the "10 Best Places for Post-docs outside the USA", by The Scientist (2010, 2011).

This event aims at creating an informal atmosphere where students share and discuss their projects with their peers, receiving precious feedback to improve their work. In addition, notable Keynote Speakers from relevant and cutting-edge biological fields are invited to enrich the event with their knowledge and advice.

The event takes place in different regions of Portugal, filled with astonishing landscapes, food and cultural landmarks.
